My Story

A quick journey of my growth, resilience, and rediscovery.

A Walk Through Time

March 2020
Pandemic Began

Lost my first post-grad job as an Executive Assistant at a counseling office. This challenge became the spark that reignited my creative direction, blending art and introspection.

October 2020
Adobe Photoshop Certified

Completed certification through NOVAC — deepening my foundation in visual design, storytelling, and digital composition.

2021 – 2022
The Film Chapter

Worked in the art and costume departments for film productions, learning to merge narrative, design, and emotion through set aesthetics and visual storytelling.

2023
Freelance Era

Launched freelance projects focused on branding, logos, and web layouts for small businesses — transforming creative intuition into professional practice.

November 2024
Google UX/UI Design Certificate

Developed expertise in human-centered design, usability testing, and accessibility. Learned to create experiences that balance empathy, clarity, and function.

February 2025
Full Stack Developer — CodeX Academy

Gained technical fluency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend frameworks. Built Inkspresso, a full e-commerce site integrating front-end design and owner-side management dashboards.

Present
Creating the Intersection

Now developing Inkspression — an interactive journaling and mindfulness web platform — while expanding my design portfolio. Focused on crafting soulful digital experiences that connect creativity, emotion, and technology.
