HER(story)

A Walk Through Time

March 2020
Pandemic Began
Lost my first post-grad job working as the Executive Administrative Assistant for a counseling office for 3 years. This moment allowed me to work on changing trajectories professionally.
October 2020
Certified
Thanks to a program in the city, New Orleans Video Access Center(NOVAC), I was able to take a workforce training and recieve a certification in Adobe Photoshop.
October 2021
A Therapist?!
I started a job working as an ABA therapist in schools, home, and clinic settings while re-evaluating what I want to do as a career.
October 2021
In The Bakground
I began taking random gigs working as an extra in some major productions being filmed around the city.
April 2022
On Set
I got my first internship as a PA on a major production
December 2022
Training Arc
During this time, I took several trainings through NOVAC in both art department and accounting.
February 2023
You're Hired!
My first job as an "artist", working as a graphic designer for The UPS store.
July 2023
Scratching & Surviving
Began working as a freelance graphic artist, creating logos and merch for small businesses amongst my friends and family.
Present
When I Grow Up
Finally getting more training and experience in the fields I want to get into, in order to build multiple streams of income using my creativity.
