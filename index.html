The Little Artist Who Was Told She Couldn't

Who Am I

My name is Ace, I'm from New Orleans, and I am a multimedia artist.

What Do I Do?

In simple terms, I create.
Graphic designs, building film sets, photography, etc.
Basically any medium I can find I will manipulate.

Why Do I Do It?

I've always had a passion for creative endeavors, but was always told
"art don't make no money."
I'm here to prove that anyone can create the life of their dreams without sacrificing creativity.
