Ace Creates: The Intersection of Art, Wellness & Design

Who Am I

I’m Chantrice “Ace” LaCabe, a multidisciplinary designer from New Orleans with roots in psychology, art, and technology.
My work bridges creativity and intention — crafting designs that nurture connection, emotion, and usability. Whether through visuals, words, or code, I aim to make experiences feel as beautiful as they are functional.

What Do I Do?

I specialize in UX/UI and visual design, blending human psychology with art and technology to create inclusive, emotionally resonant digital experiences.
My approach combines aesthetic intuition with strategic thinking — from wireframes and prototypes to brand systems and front-end builds.
I love transforming complex ideas into simple, meaningful interactions that align with both user needs and business goals.

Why Do I Do It?

I’m motivated by the belief that creativity is a form of healing and connection.
My mission is to design tools and experiences that help people express themselves, find balance, and feel seen.
Every project is an opportunity to merge functionality with feeling — to remind people that technology can be as human, gentle, and inspiring as art itself.
