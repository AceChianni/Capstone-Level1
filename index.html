Ace Creates: The Intersection of Art, Wellness & Design

Who Am I

I’m Chantrice “Ace” LaCabe, a multidisciplinary designer from New Orleans
with an extensive background in psychology, art, and technology.
My work bridges creativity and intention — crafting designs that nurture connection, emotion, and usability.

What Do I Do?

I specialize in UX/UI and visual design, blending human psychology with art and technology to create inclusive, emotionally resonant digital experiences.
My approach transforms complex ideas into simple, meaningful interactions.

Why Do I Do It?

Creativity is a form of healing and connection.
I design tools and experiences that help people express themselves, find balance, and feel seen — making technology as gentle and inspiring as art itself.
